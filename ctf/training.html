CTF Training Overview

Structured practice designed to build real technical skill while strengthening collaboration and problem-solving.

Training is divided into Q&A sessions, in-person collaboration, and independent remote practice. Bring curiosity, questions, and the willingness to experiment.

Training Philosophy

Have fun, learn naturally, and make progress together.

Practice • Collaboration • Exploration

Learn • Break • Fix • Repeat

Q&A Sessions

Bring a list of concepts or problems you don’t understand. The group works together to figure them out.

  • If the group can’t explain something, we explore tools together — AI, Google, documentation, and walkthroughs.
  • Sessions focus on clarity, not speed.

In-person Trainings

The primary goal is to enjoy the challenge and get to know your teammates. Learning happens naturally.

Save the grinding for remote practice — in-person time is for sharing, exploring, and building teamwork.

Remote Trainings (On Your Own)

Remote practice gives you time to grind, explore tools deeply, and strengthen your weaknesses.
