Educator, researcher, and longtime aerospace professional.

Jennifer empowers students and colleagues through one-on-one collaboration on technology-focused research and coursework. By building a strong digital presence, faculty create a durable legacy for their work and open doors to future opportunities.

Teaching • Research • Industry

Background

Jennifer is ABD in a Ph.D. program in Information Systems and holds an M.S. in Computer Science from the University of New Haven in West Haven, Connecticut.

Her undergraduate studies were in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Rutgers University in New Brunswick, New Jersey.

With over 25 years in the aerospace field, Jennifer has focused on structural analysis and FAA substantiation of interior structures for aircraft.
